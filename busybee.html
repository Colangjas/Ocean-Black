BUSY BEE

Platform: Android and iPhone

Available on Google Play Store

Description:

Busy Bee is an addictive 2D side-scroller where you are pitted against robots made by an evil scientist who is out to destroy all of bee kind! It’s a great game for kids, adults, and anyone that loves android games, arcade games, iPhone games, kids games, and mobile games in general. In Busy Bee you play 9 different levels of difficulty over 3 different colorful environments. Try to gather all the pollen you can to buy power-ups to go faster and blow away the evil robots.

Features

  1. FUN TO PLAY
  2. Hone your skills at dodging and collecting pollen
  3. Buy power-ups with your pollen
  4. Get all of the achievements
  5. Regular mode and survival mode

Backstory

Once upon a time, there was a boy genius who could build any invention he could imagine. The child lived on a farm and his parents were bee keepers. Lately they’ve been on hard times because the bees haven’t been making as much honey. He was also allergic to bees so he was constantly living in fear of them.

One day while looking at kitten videos on YouTube he stumbled across a video explaining how wasps were the bee’s mortal enemy. He got an idea that if he could have a wasp army he could enslave the bee population. And so he decided to enact his master plan. He immediately commenced work on a tiny army of wasp robots that would capture, torture, and enslave the bees. And so our story begins.
