BUSY BEE

Platform: Android and iPhone

Available on Google Play Store

Description:

Busy Bee is an addictive 2D side-scroller where you are pitted against robots made by an evil scientist who is out to destroy all of bee kind! It’s a great game for kids, adults, and anyone that loves android games, arcade games, iPhone games, kids games, and mobile games in general. In Busy Bee you play 9 different levels of difficulty over 3 different colorful environments. Try to gather all the pollen you can to buy power-ups to go faster and blow away the evil robots.
